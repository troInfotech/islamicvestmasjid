A Look Into Our History

Since its establishment, our institution has been a hub for the Muslim community and a home for generations of families seeking spiritual growth and community connection.

The Islamic Center serves as one of the largest mosques in the region, welcoming people of all faiths and backgrounds to learn, worship, and grow together.

  • Founded on principles of faith and service
  • Weekly community breakfast
  • Youth and family programs
  • Philanthropic initiatives
  • Welcoming to all faiths
  • Arabic school & Islamic studies
