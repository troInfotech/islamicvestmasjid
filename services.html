Our Services

Serving the community with faith, dedication, and compassion

Daily Prayers

Join us for the five daily congregational prayers in our beautiful prayer hall. Our masjid is open throughout the day for individual and congregational worship.

We maintain a welcoming atmosphere for both brothers and sisters, with separate prayer areas available.

  • Five daily prayers with congregation
  • Spacious prayer halls for men and women
  • Clean wudu facilities available
  • Handicap accessible entrance and facilities
  • Childcare available during select prayers

Jumu'ah (Friday) Prayer

The Friday congregational prayer is the highlight of our week. Join hundreds of community members for the weekly sermon and prayer.

We offer multiple Jumu'ah services to accommodate our growing community.

  • First Jumu'ah: Khutbah at 1:00 PM, Prayer at 1:30 PM
  • Second Jumu'ah: Khutbah at 2:00 PM, Prayer at 2:30 PM
  • Inspiring khutbahs by our resident scholars
  • Topics relevant to contemporary Muslim life
  • Overflow area with live audio/video

Islamic Education

We offer comprehensive Islamic education programs for all ages, from young children to adults. Our qualified teachers provide instruction in Quran, Arabic, and Islamic studies.

Weekend Islamic School

Saturdays, 10:30 AM - 2:00 PM

Arabic language and Islamic studies for children ages 4-15.

Quran Memorization (Hifz)

Daily, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Structured program for children and adults to memorize the Holy Quran.

Adult Islamic Studies

Mon & Wed, 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Comprehensive courses on Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Tafsir for ages 16+.

Quran Tafsir

Saturdays, 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Deep dive into the meanings and interpretations of the Holy Quran.

Funeral Services

During times of loss, our community comes together to support grieving families. We provide complete Islamic funeral services with compassion and dignity.

  • Ghusl (ritual washing) performed by trained volunteers
  • Kafan (shrouding) provided
  • Janazah prayer at the masjid
  • Coordination with local Muslim cemeteries
  • Bereavement support and counseling
  • Memorial gatherings and condolence services
  • 24/7 emergency assistance available

Marriage Services

We celebrate the blessed union of marriage in accordance with Islamic traditions. Our Imam performs Nikah ceremonies and provides pre-marital counseling.

  • Nikah (Islamic marriage) ceremonies
  • Pre-marital counseling sessions
  • Islamic marriage certificates
  • Venue available for walima (reception)
  • Marriage counseling for couples
  • Family mediation services
