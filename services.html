Daily Prayers

Join us for the five daily congregational prayers in our beautiful prayer hall. Our masjid is open throughout the day for individual and congregational worship.

We maintain a welcoming atmosphere for both brothers and sisters, with separate prayer areas available.

  • Five daily prayers with congregation
  • Spacious prayer halls for men and women
  • Clean wudu facilities available
  • Handicap accessible entrance and facilities
  • Childcare available during select prayers

Jumu'ah (Friday) Prayer

The Friday congregational prayer is the highlight of our week. Join hundreds of community members for the weekly sermon and prayer.

We offer multiple Jumu'ah services to accommodate our growing community.

  • First Jumu'ah: Khutbah at 1:00 PM, Prayer at 1:30 PM
  • Second Jumu'ah: Khutbah at 2:00 PM, Prayer at 2:30 PM
  • Inspiring khutbahs by our resident scholars
  • Topics relevant to contemporary Muslim life
  • Overflow area with live audio/video

Qur'an Academy

Our flagship Qur'an Academy offers structured programs for learning to read, understand, and memorize the Holy Qur'an. We use proven teaching methods with qualified instructors.

Classes are available for children, teens, and adults at all skill levels - from beginners learning Arabic letters to advanced students perfecting Tajweed.

  • Qaida & Tajweed: Learn proper pronunciation and recitation rules
  • Hifz Program: Memorization with weekly review sessions
  • Tafseer Classes: Understanding the meanings and context
  • One-on-One Sessions: Personal instruction available
  • Weekend & Evening Options: Flexible scheduling
  • Online Classes: Distance learning for those unable to attend in person

Registration open year-round. New students welcome at any time!

Madrasah (Islamic School)

Our weekend Madrasah provides comprehensive Islamic education for children ages 4-16 in a fun, engaging environment. Classes run every Saturday and Sunday.

Students learn Arabic language, Islamic history, Fiqh, Aqeedah, character development, and memorization of essential Duas and Surahs.

Level 1 (Ages 4-6)

Sat & Sun, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Islamic basics, Arabic alphabet, simple duas, and prophets' stories.

Level 2 (Ages 7-10)

Sat & Sun, 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM

Quran reading, Islamic studies, basic Arabic, and character building.

Level 3 (Ages 11-13)

Sat & Sun, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Advanced Quran, Fiqh, Seerah, and Islamic civilization history.

Level 4 (Ages 14-16)

Sundays, 4:30 PM - 6:30 PM

Islamic jurisprudence, contemporary issues, and leadership training.

Funeral Services

During times of loss, our community comes together to support grieving families. We provide complete Islamic funeral services with compassion and dignity.

  • Ghusl (ritual washing) performed by trained volunteers
  • Kafan (shrouding) provided
  • Janazah prayer at the masjid
  • Coordination with local Muslim cemeteries
  • Bereavement support and counseling
  • Memorial gatherings and condolence services
  • 24/7 emergency assistance available

Community Advice & Counseling

Life can be challenging, and we're here to help. Our Imam and trained counselors provide confidential guidance on religious, personal, and family matters.

Whether you need Islamic rulings on specific issues, marriage counseling, or just someone to talk to, we offer a compassionate ear and practical solutions rooted in Islamic principles.

  • Religious Guidance: Fatwa and Islamic rulings on daily matters
  • Marriage & Family Counseling: Professional help for couples and families
  • Youth Mentoring: Guidance for teenagers and young adults
  • Financial Advice: Islamic perspective on money matters
  • Career Guidance: Balancing career with Islamic values
  • Mental Health Support: Referrals to Muslim mental health professionals
  • Conflict Resolution: Mediation services for disputes

All consultations are completely confidential. Schedule by appointment.

New Muslim Support

Embracing Islam is a beautiful journey, and we're here to support you every step of the way. Our New Muslim Support Program provides guidance, education, and community.

Whether you're considering Islam or recently took your Shahada, we offer personalized mentorship and resources to help you grow in faith.

  • Shahada Ceremony: Taking the testimony of faith with witnesses
  • New Muslim Classes: Weekly classes covering Islamic basics
  • One-on-One Mentorship: Paired with experienced Muslim mentors
  • Learn to Pray: Step-by-step prayer instruction
  • Quran Basics: Learning to read Arabic and understand the Quran
  • Social Integration: Welcome events and community connections
  • Resource Library: Books, videos, and materials for new Muslims

Community Events & Activities

Throughout the year, we host various events that bring our community together in worship, celebration, and service. These gatherings strengthen our bonds and create lasting memories.

From Eid celebrations to charity drives, educational lectures to family fun days - there's always something happening at our center!

  • Eid Celebrations: Grand celebrations for Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha
  • Ramadan Programs: Taraweeh prayers, Iftar dinners, and I'tikaf
  • Islamic Heritage Month: Cultural celebrations and educational programs
  • Guest Speakers: Regular visits from renowned Islamic scholars
  • Community Iftars: Monthly community dinners throughout the year
  • Charity Drives: Food collections, clothing drives, and fundraising events
  • Family Fun Days: Picnics, sports tournaments, and recreational activities
  • Interfaith Dialogues: Building bridges with other faith communities

Stay updated on upcoming events through our newsletter and social media!
